Mood Map is a comprehensive, free, and open-source tool designed to help people understand and navigate their emotional landscape. It offers a visual representation of their emotions, along with suggestions for self-care and support. Mood Map is a powerful tool that can help individuals identify their emotional state, improve their mood, and manage their stress and anxiety.At Mood Map, we believe taking care of your mind should be easy, accessible, and compassionate—because your well-being matters
